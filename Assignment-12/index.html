About Sentry, the Torino Scale & the Palermo Scale

Sentry is JPL's automated impact monitoring system that continuously scans the catalog of known near-Earth objects to detect any possibilities of future Earth impact over the next 100 years.

Torino Scale (0–10): 0 = no hazard, 10 = certain collision with global devastation. Most objects are rated 0. A rating of 1 merits monitoring.

Palermo Scale: negative = below background risk, positive = above background risk. Values below −2 are routine; values above 0 warrant heightened attention.
